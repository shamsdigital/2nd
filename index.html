With rapidly growing lead volume, the sales team was stretched to its limit. Responding to every inbound inquiry while trying to nurture customer relationships was becoming increasingly difficult, Too much time was being spent answering basic questions and chasing unresponsive leads, preventing sales reps from focusing on closing deals.
